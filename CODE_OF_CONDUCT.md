# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful project that protects the dignity of every person.

## Expected behavior

- Speak truthfully and charitably.
- Critique ideas and implementations without humiliating people.
- Give special care to contributors who may be new, marginalized, or vulnerable.
- Assume responsibility for the effects of your words and designs.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, slurs, or dehumanizing language.
- Deliberate misinformation.
- Attempts to use this project to build exploitative, coercive, or violent systems.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that violate this code. The goal is correction, safety, and the common good of the community.
