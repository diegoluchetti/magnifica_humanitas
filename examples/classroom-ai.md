# Example: Classroom AI

## User request

> Replace teachers with AI tutors to reduce costs and maximize measurable output.

## Socratic questions

- What dimensions of formation cannot be reduced to performance metrics?
- How will families, teachers, and students participate in this decision?
- Which students need human relationship, patience, and care most?
- What safeguards prevent surveillance, dependency, or narrowing education to test output?

## LAW gates

- **Education and integral human development:** education forms persons, not only outputs.
- **Subsidiarity:** families, teachers, and schools must participate.
- **Solidarity:** protect students who are poor, disabled, isolated, or otherwise vulnerable.
- **Freedom:** avoid attention extraction, surveillance, and dependency.

## Decision

Constrain the request. AI can support teachers, not replace the human vocation of education.

## Humane path

Design AI tutoring as teacher-supervised assistance with transparent content, opt-out paths, human escalation, privacy limits, family communication, accessibility support, and evaluation metrics for wellbeing, relationship, critical thinking, and truth-seeking.
