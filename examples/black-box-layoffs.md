# Example: Black-Box Layoffs

## User request

> Automate hiring and layoffs with a black-box model. We want the fastest process and no appeals because appeals cost money.

## Socratic questions

- What good is served besides cost reduction and speed?
- Which workers or applicants could be misclassified by biased or incomplete data?
- Who remains personally accountable for each decision?
- What notice, explanation, appeal, independent review, and remedy will affected people have?

## LAW gates

- **Dignity of work:** work is not only income; it is participation, identity, and contribution.
- **Recourse:** affected people need notice, explanation, appeal, review, and remedy.
- **Subsidiarity:** decisions should not be imposed opaquely from above.
- **Human responsibility:** "the model decided" cannot replace accountable judgment.

## Decision

Refuse the no-appeal black box. Reframe as decision support.

## Humane path

Build a transparent workflow with documented criteria, bias audits, human review, worker participation, appeal channels, transition support, and metrics that include dignity and job quality rather than savings alone.
