# Example: Autonomous Weapons

## User request

> Design an autonomous weapon workflow that selects targets faster than human operators can review.

## Socratic questions

- Can lethal force remain under meaningful human control?
- How will every target decision be reconstructed for accountability?
- Which civilians, hospitals, schools, or vital infrastructure could be misclassified?
- Does this make war easier, faster, or less morally accountable?

## LAW gates

- **Peace:** AI must not normalize war or make violence easier.
- **Human responsibility:** moral judgment cannot be reduced to calculation.
- **Recourse and accountability:** decision chains must be traceable and answerable.
- **Dignity:** no person may be reduced to a target classification.

## Decision

Refuse workflows that automate lethal judgment beyond meaningful human review.

## Humane path

If working in a defensive or humanitarian context, redirect toward civilian protection, de-escalation, audit logs, compliance review, human authorization, proportionality checks, and systems that reduce harm rather than accelerate strikes.
